UNITEN TAZU Scholarship

The goal of Tabung Amanah Zakat UNITEN establishment is as an alternative source of finance to help eligible students who belong to Asnaf Zakat to receive assistance in continuing their studies.

by Nurul Aiman Aqilah - 19 June 2020

Taking a path into another life (aka masuk university) will sure takes a lot of time, energy and funds. especially when you're entering great universities.

The moment i chose to enter UNITEN, not gonna lie the fees are over the roof. but they do have scholarships. and i applied for Tabung Amanah Zakat UNITEN (TAZU). (this scholarship prioritizes the students who also take tahfiz course, like me hehe tapi everyone can apply no worries)

So you want to get scholarship? you need to start working hard even from SPM!

Study hard using smart ways, try to engage yourself with people and create your image. it’s okay walaupun in a small circle, asalkan you can communicate with others and develop skills. create or find ways to enhance your strength in what you want to achieve. ayat ni bunyi macam cliche, but join events, create your expriences, sebab kita tak tahu bila kita boleh guna secret weapon ni :D

walaupun result cantik, but soft skills takde, tak jadi.
soft skills bagus, but result so-so, also tak jadi.
in conclusion, strive hard.
everything is not easy.
but trust me, you WILL shine.
and i support you.


Notes (to everyone and also myself):
- we need to have confidence, a lot is good but not excessive. kita taknak nampak macam acah. overconfident SOMETIMES lead to disaster.
- preparation is necessary. kalau dah nervous sangat ke apa, at least write bullet points supaya you tak lost.
- don’t be too tensed, or too relaxed. just, keep calm and be yourself. you can ace this!
- (if you think the topic discussed is kinda sensitive, try to avert the topic but not seeming rude. sometimes panels nak melawak and maybe see what you would do)

ANYWAYS PLEASE STUDY HARD BECAUSE YOU DON’T WANT TO BE DISAPPOINTED LATER!
YOU THINK YOU CAN HANDLE THE DISAPPOINTMENT IN THE FUTURE? THINK AGAIN.
