Shell Scholarship

Since its inception, the Shell Malaysia Scholarship Programme has benefitted more than 1500 students in pursuing their educational aspirations and provided a unique entrance into the energy industry. Learn from Aisyah's experience applying this prestigious scholarship.

by Aisyah Maisarah - 9 June 2020

hi.
After SPM and if you're aiming to get a scholarship, improve your English and Math, do practice verbal reasoning and numerical reasoning tests. Almost all scholarship will prepare these tests for you (saringan 2nd stage). In my opinion, stage ni susah :(

Jangan lupa apa yang dah belajar masa SPM. Certain scholarship ada exam that involves topics from SPM. Certain university ada entrance exam guna soalan SPM juga. Latihan SPM tu buat je sementara tunggu UPU, nanti tak rasa karat bila Pre-U dah start.

Saringan nak dapat scholarship, even if anda straight 11As, is very competitive. Academic excellence, involvement, good personality, confidence, critical thinking. Those are what they're trying to find in you through the assesments, interviews and case analysis.

Here, my mistake was being too nervous if I'll be doing not good enough. I've prepared complete script for an interview dan hafal. So I've learnt my lesson, dengan perfect preparation, buat lagi nervous kalau sebut tak ikut script (valid for interview only). I stuttered and messed up. Interview ni best kalau kita bercerita naturally. Prepare tu bagus dah tapi buat dalam bentuk point is better.

So the biggest lessons I've acquired from rejections ni ialah; RELAX, BE ORIGINAL AND BELIEVE IN YOURSELF.

sounds cliche but this time it really does hits me deeply and I'll improve!

Doakan ecah dapat scholarship untuk Degree :)

Best of luck in your future endeavours, Abqarians!
